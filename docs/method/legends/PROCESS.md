# Legend: PROCESS

The mechanics of METHOD itself: cycle discipline, backlog operations,
drift detection, and named patterns for how work moves through the
system.

## What it covers

- cycle design and closeout rules
- drift detection and traceability checks
- backlog maintenance and legend operations
- conventions for spikes, pivots, and other work patterns
- tooling that makes METHOD's repo workflow cheaper and more reliable

## What success looks like

A METHOD repo stays calm and legible under real use. The work loop is
easy to follow, maintenance is cheap, and the process generates better
next moves instead of clerical thrash.

## How you know

- active work and next work are easy to discover
- drift is caught before or at closeout, not after confusion spreads
- backlog maintenance operations are visible and low-friction
- temporary spikes and pivots are documented honestly instead of being
  confused with failure or abandoned work
